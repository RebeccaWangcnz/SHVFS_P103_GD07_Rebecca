1. This is a local multi-player game. There are 2 teams or more.
2. Each teams need to collect food in the map and take the food back to their own team's zone. They can go into other's zones 
and carry their food out.
3. There is limited time. The game will show the scores of each teams. 
The team with the highest scores wins.
4. The food will appear randomly .There are good food and bad food.
6. There is prop players can obtain and use. 
	       Acceleration: Player can move faster than others in a limited time.
